# Ownership Costs

Owning an E46 330 is rewarding but requires honest budgeting. These are not Camrys â€” parts and labor costs reflect the BMW engineering underneath.

## Annual Maintenance Budget

### DIY Owner
- Routine maintenance (oil, filters, fluids): $300-$500/year
- Wear items (brakes, tires): $500-$800/year
- Unexpected repairs: $500-$1,500/year
- **Total: $1,300-$2,800/year**

### Independent Shop
- Routine maintenance: $600-$1,000/year
- Wear items: $800-$1,200/year
- Unexpected repairs: $1,000-$3,000/year
- **Total: $2,400-$5,200/year**

### BMW Dealer
- Routine maintenance: $1,200-$2,000/year
- Wear items: $1,500-$2,500/year
- Unexpected repairs: $2,000-$5,000/year
- **Total: $4,700-$9,500/year** (not recommended for a 20+ year old car)
