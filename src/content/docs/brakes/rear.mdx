# Rear Brakes

The E46 330 rear brakes use 320x20mm ventilated rotors. The rear also incorporates a drum-in-disc parking brake mechanism.

## Pad and Rotor Replacement

Similar to front brakes with the following differences:
- Rear caliper carrier bolts: 65 Nm
- Rear caliper guide pin bolts: 30 Nm
- Rear piston may require rotation tool to compress (piston screws in)
- Parking brake shoes must be adjusted after rotor replacement

## Parking Brake Shoes

When replacing rear rotors, inspect parking brake shoes:
- Remove rotor to access shoes
- Check shoe thickness (minimum 1.5mm lining)
- Adjust star wheel for proper clearance after assembly

## Rear Brake Specifications

- Rotor: 320x20mm ventilated
- Minimum thickness: 17.4mm
- Caliper: Single-piston floating
