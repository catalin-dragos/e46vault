# Technical Service Bulletins & Recalls

- **Brake light switch**: Some model years recalled for faulty brake light switches

## Common TSBs

### Cooling System
- TSB regarding expansion tank cracking and cooling system component replacement intervals
- Water pump impeller failures addressed in service notifications

### VANOS
- Service bulletin regarding VANOS rattle and updated seal kits

### Rear Subframe
- BMW issued extended warranty coverage for subframe mount cracking on certain model years and VIN ranges

### Electrical
- Window regulator cable failure TSB with updated part design
- Blower motor FSR updated part with improved thermal design

## Checking Your Vehicle

- Enter your VIN at [NHTSA Recalls](https://www.nhtsa.gov/recalls) for US recalls
- Contact your local BMW dealer for TSB coverage
- TSBs are not mandatory recalls but address known issues
