# Fuse Chart

The E46 has two fuse boxes: one in the glove box area and one in the engine bay.

## Glove Box Fuse Panel

Located behind the glove box door, lower right dash area. Contains 31 fuses.

Refer to the fuse chart data for complete positions, ratings, and circuit descriptions.

## Engine Bay Fuse Box

Located on the right side of the engine bay, behind the strut tower. Contains 8 large fuses (maxi fuses).

## Troubleshooting Tips

- Always replace fuses with the correct amperage rating
- If a fuse blows repeatedly, diagnose the underlying short circuit
- Use a test light or multimeter to check fuses without removing them
- Keep spare fuses in the glove box (there's a built-in holder)
