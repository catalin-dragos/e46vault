# Oil Leaks

As the E46 ages, oil leaks become increasingly common. Here are the most frequent sources.

## Common Leak Locations

### Valve Cover Gasket
- **Symptoms**: Oil seepage on sides of engine, burning oil smell
- **Part**: 11 12 7 588 412 (includes spark plug tube seals)
- **Torque**: 10 Nm (aluminum — do not overtighten)

### Oil Filter Housing Gasket
- **Symptoms**: Oil drip from front-center of engine
- **Gasket**: Located between oil filter housing and engine block
- **Common to leak after careless filter changes

### Oil Pan Gasket
- **Symptoms**: Oil drip from bottom of engine, oil on subframe
- **Part**: 11 13 7 548 031
- **Repair**: Requires subframe lowering or removal for access

### VANOS Solenoid Seals
- **Symptoms**: Oil on top of valve cover near VANOS solenoids
- **O-rings harden and leak with age

### Rear Main Seal
- **Symptoms**: Oil between engine and transmission
- **Repair**: Requires transmission removal — often done with clutch replacement

## Prevention
- Regular oil changes with quality oil and filter
- Don't overfill oil level
- Replace gaskets proactively at major service intervals
