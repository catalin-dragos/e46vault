# Alternator Failure

The alternator charges the battery and powers electrical systems. E46 alternators typically last 100,000-150,000 miles.

## Symptoms
- Battery warning light on dashboard
- Dimming headlights
- Electrical accessories malfunctioning
- Difficulty starting
- Battery not holding charge
- Whining noise from alternator

## Diagnosis
- Check charging voltage: Should be 13.8-14.4V with engine running
- Load test alternator output
- Check belt tension and condition
- Inspect alternator connections for corrosion

## Replacement
The alternator is located on the front of the engine, driven by the serpentine belt. Part: 12 31 7 515 030 (120A)
