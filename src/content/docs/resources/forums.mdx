# Forums & Communities

## Dedicated E46 Forums
- **E46Fanatics.com**: The largest E46-specific forum with decades of archived knowledge
- **Bimmerforums.com**: Active BMW community with E46 subforum
- **R3VLimited.com**: E46 M3 focused but applicable to all E46

## Reddit Communities
- **r/e46**: Active community for all E46 models
- **r/BMW**: General BMW subreddit
- **r/BmwTech**: Technical questions and troubleshooting

- BMW E46 Parts & Sales
