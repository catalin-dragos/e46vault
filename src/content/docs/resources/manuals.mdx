# Repair Manuals & References

## Factory Resources
- **BMW TIS (Technical Information System)**: Factory repair procedures, wiring diagrams, TSBs
- **BMW ETK (Electronic Parts Catalog)**: Official parts lookup with exploded diagrams
- **RealOEM.com**: Free web-based ETK alternative

## Aftermarket Manuals
- **Bentley Publishers BMW E46 Service Manual**: Comprehensive, considered the DIY bible
- **Haynes BMW 3-Series Manual**: Budget-friendly, covers basics
- **Chilton BMW Manual**: Alternative to Haynes

## Digital Resources
- **Newtis.info**: Free online BMW TIS access
- **E46Fanatics Wiki**: Community-maintained repair guides
- **YouTube**: See YouTube Channels resource page

## Recommended Reading Order for New Owners
1. Bentley manual — understanding your specific systems
2. E46Fanatics FAQ section — common issues and solutions
3. BMW TIS — for specific repair procedures and torque specs
4. This knowledge base — for organized, verified reference
