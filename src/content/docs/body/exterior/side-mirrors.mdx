# Side Mirrors

## Features (Depending on Options)
- Power adjustment
- Heated mirrors
- Power folding (some markets)
- Auto-dimming (driver side)
- Integrated turn signals (facelift models, some trims)

## Mirror Glass Replacement
The mirror glass can be replaced independently of the mirror housing:
1. Tilt mirror fully inward
2. Pry glass off backing plate (carefully)
3. Disconnect heating element wire (if equipped)
4. Connect new glass and press onto backing plate

## Complete Mirror Replacement
Requires door panel removal to access mounting bolts and wiring connector.
