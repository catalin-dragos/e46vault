# Doors

## Door Components
- Door shell (steel)
- Door panel (interior trim)
- Window regulator and motor
- Door lock actuator
- Side mirror mounting
- Speaker mounting
- Vapor barrier (moisture protection)
- Wiring harness (through door boot)

## Common Issues
- Window regulator failure (see Common Problems)
- Door lock actuator failure
- Door boot wiring breakage (pinched wires)
- Sagging door (worn hinge pins)

## Door Hinge Repair
Sagging doors are caused by worn hinge pins. BMW recommends replacing the entire hinge, but hinge pin kits are available for repair.
