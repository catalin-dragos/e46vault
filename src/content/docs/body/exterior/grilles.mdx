# Kidney Grilles

The BMW kidney grilles are a signature design element. E46 grilles are a quick, easy upgrade.

## OEM Options
- Chrome surround with black slats (standard)
- Titanium/matte surround (some trims)

## Aftermarket Options
- Gloss black (most popular upgrade)
- Matte black
- Carbon fiber
- Color-matched to body

## Replacement
1. Open hood
2. Press retaining clips from behind
3. Pull grilles forward to remove
4. Snap new grilles into place
5. No tools required
