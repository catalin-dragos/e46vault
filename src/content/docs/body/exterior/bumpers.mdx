# Bumpers

The E46 bumper covers are painted plastic. Pre-facelift and facelift bumpers have different designs and are not directly interchangeable.

## Front Bumper Options
- Standard bumper (pre-facelift and facelift)
- M-Sport (M-Tech II) bumper — more aggressive styling, popular upgrade
- ZHP bumper — M-Tech II with fog lights

## Rear Bumper
- Standard rear bumper with parking sensor provisions
- M-Sport rear bumper with diffuser styling
- ZHP rear bumper with quad exhaust cutouts

## Removal
1. Remove wheel arch liner screws (front sections)
2. Remove undertray screws
3. Remove bumper-to-fender screws
4. Disconnect fog lights and parking sensors
5. Pull bumper cover forward/rearward
