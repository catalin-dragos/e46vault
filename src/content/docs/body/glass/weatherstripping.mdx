# Weatherstripping

## Common Weatherstrip Locations
- Door seals (on door and on body)
- Window channel seals
- Trunk seal
- Windshield trim
- Rear window seal
- Convertible top seal

## Maintenance
- Clean with rubber conditioner
- Apply silicone lubricant to prevent sticking
- Replace if cracked, torn, or leaking
- Water leaks often caused by deteriorated weatherstripping
- Check drain holes in doors and trunk are clear
