# Interior Trim Removal

## Essential Tools
- Plastic trim removal tools (avoid metal to prevent scratches)
- Phillips screwdriver
- T20/T25 Torx drivers
- Small flathead for prying clips

## A-Pillar Trim
- Pull firmly at bottom to pop clips
- Disconnect tweeter speaker wire (if equipped)

## B-Pillar Trim
- Remove seat belt bolt cover
- Pull trim to release clips

## C-Pillar Trim (Sedan)
- Remove rear shelf side trim first
- Pull to release clips

## Kick Panel / Footwell Trim
- Twist retaining fasteners 90Â°
- Pull panel down and forward
- Access fuse box and footwell modules

## General Tips
- Work in warm temperatures (clips are less brittle)
- Have spare clips available (they break easily)
- Take photos before removal for reassembly reference
