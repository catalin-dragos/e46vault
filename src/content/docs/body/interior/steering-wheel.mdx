# Steering Wheel

See the Steering section for detailed steering wheel information, removal procedure, and upgrade options.

## Quick Reference Options
- Standard 3-spoke
- M-Sport 3-spoke (thicker rim, M-stitching)
- Multifunction (with audio controls)
- ZHP illuminated M-Sport wheel

## Popular Upgrade
The M-Sport steering wheel is the most common interior upgrade for non-M-Sport E46 models. Direct fit with no adapter needed.
