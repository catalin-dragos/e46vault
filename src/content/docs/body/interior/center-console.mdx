# Center Console

## Components
- Shift boot and trim plate
- Cup holders
- Storage compartment
- Parking brake lever and boot
- Climate control panel
- Radio/navigation head unit
- Armrest (if equipped)

## Common Issues
- Cup holder mechanism breakage
- Shift boot tearing
- Armrest hinge wear
- Center console trim scratches
- Ashtray lighter socket failure

## Trim Options
- Standard wood trim (various patterns by year)
- Aluminum trim (sport packages)
- Carbon fiber (aftermarket)
- Alcantara (ZHP)
