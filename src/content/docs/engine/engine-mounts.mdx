# Engine Mounts

The M54 uses two hydraulic engine mounts and one transmission mount. They isolate engine vibration from the chassis.

## Symptoms of Worn Mounts
- Excessive engine movement visible
- Vibration at idle transmitted to cabin
- Clunking during acceleration or deceleration
- Visible fluid leak from mount (hydraulic type)

## Engine Mounts (2x)
- Located on either side of engine block
- Hydraulic fluid-filled rubber mounts
- Replace in pairs

## Transmission Mount
- Located under transmission tail
- Rubber mount with metal bracket
- Torque: 45 Nm

## Upgrade Options
- Condor Speed Shop solid mounts (track use)
- Polyurethane mounts (compromise between stock and solid)
- Solid mounts significantly increase NVH
