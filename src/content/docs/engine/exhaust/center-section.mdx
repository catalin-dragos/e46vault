# Center Section & Resonator

The center section connects the catalytic converter to the rear muffler and contains a resonator to control exhaust noise.

## Common Issues
- Resonator corrosion and perforation
- Exhaust hanger breakage
- Gasket leaks at connection points
- Exhaust drone (if resonator is deleted or damaged)
