# Exhaust Hangers & Gaskets

## Exhaust Hangers
- Rubber isolator mounts connecting exhaust to body
- Replace if cracked, stretched, or missing
- Symptoms: Exhaust rattling against body, sagging exhaust

## Exhaust Gaskets
- Replace at every exhaust component removal
- Manifold gaskets, cat gaskets, muffler gaskets
- Use OEM or quality aftermarket (Victor Reinz)
- Manifold nuts: 20 Nm
