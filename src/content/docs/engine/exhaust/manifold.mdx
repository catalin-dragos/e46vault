# Exhaust Manifold

The M54 uses a cast iron exhaust manifold (headers) with two sections merging into a single downpipe.

## Specifications
- Material: Cast iron
- Configuration: 6-into-2-into-1
- Exhaust manifold nut torque: 20 Nm

## Common Issues
- Cracked manifold (heat cycling)
- Exhaust manifold stud corrosion/breakage
- Gasket leaks (ticking sound on cold start)
- Heat shield rattle
