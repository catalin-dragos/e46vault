# Fuel Pump

The E46 uses an in-tank electric fuel pump to deliver fuel to the engine at approximately 3.5 bar (50 PSI).

## Symptoms of Failure
- Engine cranks but won't start
- Stalling or hesitation under load
- Whining noise from fuel tank area
- Loss of power at high RPM
- Long cranking before starting

## Diagnosis
- Listen for pump priming when key is turned to position 2
- Check fuel pressure at rail (should be ~3.5 bar)
- Scan for fuel pressure-related fault codes

## Replacement
- Fuel pump is accessed through panel under rear seat
- Replace fuel filter at same time
- Replace O-ring seal on sender unit
