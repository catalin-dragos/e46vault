# Coolant Hoses

Rubber coolant hoses deteriorate with age and heat. Inspect regularly and replace proactively.

## Key Hoses
- Upper radiator hose: 17 11 1 436 251
- Lower radiator hose: 17 12 7 510 952
- Heater hoses (multiple)
- Expansion tank hoses

## Inspection
- Squeeze hoses: Should be firm, not soft or spongy
- Check for cracks, swelling, or bulging
- Look for coolant residue at connections
- Replace any hose that feels soft or shows deterioration
