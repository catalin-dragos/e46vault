# Radiator

The E46 radiator has an aluminum core with plastic end tanks. The plastic tanks can crack with age and heat cycling.

## Part Numbers
- Manual transmission: 17 11 7 573 781
- Automatic transmission: 17 11 7 573 784 (includes ATF cooler lines)

## Symptoms of Failure
- Coolant leak from end tanks (top or bottom)
- Brown or discolored coolant (internal corrosion)
- Overheating due to restricted flow
- Cracks visible on plastic tanks

## Recommended Brands
- Behr (OEM supplier)
- Nissens (quality aftermarket)
- Mishimoto (aluminum upgrade for performance)
