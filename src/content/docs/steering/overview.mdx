# Steering Overview

The BMW E46 uses a hydraulic power-assisted rack-and-pinion steering system. The system provides excellent road feel characteristic of BMW's driving dynamics.

## System Components

- **Steering rack**: ZF rack-and-pinion unit
- **Power steering pump**: Belt-driven vane pump
- **Steering column**: Collapsible with tilt adjustment
- **Steering wheel**: 3-spoke or M-Sport options
- **Tie rods**: Inner and outer, connect rack to knuckles
- **Fluid**: ATF Dexron III (most USA-market) or Pentosin CHF 11S (some European-market)

## Steering Ratio

- Standard: 16.1:1
- Lock-to-lock: 3.2 turns
- Turning circle: 10.5m (34.4 ft)
