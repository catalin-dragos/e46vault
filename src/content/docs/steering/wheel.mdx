# Steering Wheel

The E46 came with several steering wheel options from factory, and many owners upgrade to M-Sport or aftermarket wheels.

## Factory Options

- **Standard 3-spoke**: Base model, non-M
- **M-Sport 3-spoke**: Thicker rim, M-stitching, thumb rests
- **Multifunction**: With audio/phone controls (some trims)

## Steering Wheel Removal

**WARNING**: Disconnect battery and wait 10+ minutes before working on steering wheel (airbag).

1. Disconnect negative battery terminal
2. Wait at least 10 minutes for airbag capacitor discharge
3. Insert Allen key into airbag release holes (behind wheel)
4. Carefully remove airbag module
5. Disconnect airbag connector
6. Remove center bolt (63 Nm)
