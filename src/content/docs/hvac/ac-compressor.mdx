# A/C Compressor

The E46 uses a Denso or Behr A/C compressor driven by the serpentine belt.

## Part Number
64 52 6 918 751 (Denso OEM)

## Specifications
- Refrigerant: R134a
- System capacity: 750g (26.5 oz)
- PAG oil: 135ml PAG 46

## Signs of Failure
- No cold air
- Compressor clutch not engaging
- Unusual noise from compressor
- Clutch slipping

## Diagnosis
1. Check if compressor clutch engages when A/C is turned on
2. If clutch doesn't engage: Check fuse, relay, pressure switch, and clutch gap
3. If clutch engages but no cold air: Check refrigerant charge
4. Metallic noise: Internal compressor failure â€” requires replacement

## Note
When replacing a compressor, also replace the receiver/drier and flush the system.
