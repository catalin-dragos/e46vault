# Rear Shocks & Springs

The E46 rear suspension uses separate shock absorbers and coil springs in a multi-link configuration.

## OEM Shock Options

- **Sachs**: OEM supplier, stock ride quality
- **Bilstein B4**: OEM replacement
- **Bilstein B6 (HD)**: Performance upgrade, firmer
- **Bilstein B8**: Short-body for lowered applications

## Replacement Procedure

1. Raise rear of vehicle, support on jack stands
2. Remove rear wheel
3. Support trailing arm with a jack
4. Remove lower shock bolt (100 Nm)
5. From trunk, remove upper shock mount nuts (piston rod nut: 14 Nm, mount-to-body nuts: 28 Nm)
6. Lower jack slightly and remove shock
7. Install new shock in reverse order
8. Springs: Requires spring compressor for separate replacement
