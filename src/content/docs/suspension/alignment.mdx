# Wheel Alignment

Proper alignment is critical for tire wear, handling, and safety. The E46 offers limited factory adjustability but aftermarket solutions expand adjustment range.

## Factory Specifications

### Front Axle
- **Camber**: -0°30' ± 30'
- **Caster**: +7°30' ± 30'
- **Toe (per side)**: +0°05' ± 5' (slight toe-in)
- **Total toe**: +0°10' ± 10'

### Rear Axle
- **Camber**: -1°50' ± 20'
- **Toe (per side)**: +0°10' ± 10' (toe-in)
- **Total toe**: +0°20' ± 10'

## Adjustment Points

The factory alignment adjustability is limited:
- **Front camber**: Eccentric bolt on strut-to-knuckle connection
- **Front toe**: Tie rod adjustment
- **Front caster**: Not adjustable (set by subframe position)
- **Rear camber**: Eccentric bolt on upper control arm
- **Rear toe**: Eccentric bolt on toe link

## Recommended Street/Spirited Settings

For improved handling while maintaining reasonable tire wear:
- Front camber: -1.0° to -1.5°
- Rear camber: -1.5° to -2.0°
- Front toe: 0° to +0°05' per side
- Rear toe: +0°10' per side
