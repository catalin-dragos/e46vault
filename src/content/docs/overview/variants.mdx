# E46 330 Variants

The BMW E46 330 was available in multiple body styles and configurations.

## Body Styles

| Model | Body | Doors | Production |
|-------|------|-------|-----------|
| 330i | Sedan | 4 | 1999-2005 |
| 330Ci | Coupe | 2 | 2000-2006 |
| 330Ci | Convertible | 2 | 2000-2006 |
| 330xi | Sedan (AWD) | 4 | 2001-2005 |

## Engine

All E46 330 variants share the M54B30 inline-6 engine:
- 3.0-liter displacement (2,979 cc)
- 225 hp @ 5,900 RPM
- 214 lb-ft @ 3,500 RPM (290 Nm)
- Dual VANOS variable valve timing

## Transmission Options

- **5-speed manual** (Getrag S5D 320Z) — standard
- **6-speed manual** (Getrag S6S 420G) — ZHP only
- **5-speed automatic** (GM 5L40-E or ZF 5HP19)

## Special Variants

### 330 ZHP (2003-2006)
The ZHP Performance Package was exclusive to North America. See the dedicated ZHP page for details.

### 330xi
The all-wheel drive variant uses BMW's xDrive predecessor system. Some drivetrain components differ from the RWD models.

## Key Differences Between Body Styles

- **Sedan**: Most practical, stiffest chassis
- **Coupe**: Slightly sportier, frameless windows, different rear suspension geometry
- **Convertible**: Hydraulic soft top, added weight (~150 lbs), reduced trunk space, same drivetrain
