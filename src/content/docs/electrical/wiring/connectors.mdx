The E46 uses various connector types throughout the vehicle. Understanding connector types helps with diagnosis and repair.

## Common Connector Types

- **Black round connectors**: Engine sensors and actuators
- **Green/gray rectangular connectors**: Body modules
- **White multi-pin connectors**: Instrument cluster and large module connectors
- **Blue connector**: OBD-II port
- **Yellow connectors**: Airbag system (SRS) â€” do not disconnect with battery connected

## Connector Issues

- Pin corrosion from moisture ingress
- Terminal back-out from vibration
- Melted connectors from overheating (especially at tail light assemblies)
- Broken locking tabs

## Repair

BMW-specific connector pins and housings are available from specialized suppliers. Use proper pin removal tools to avoid damaging connectors.
