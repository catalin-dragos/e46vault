The E46 has two main fuse locations:

## 1. Glove Box Fuse Panel

- **Location**: Behind the glove box door, on the right side of the dashboard
- **Access**: Open glove box, push in the sides to release the stops, lower the door fully
- **Contents**: ~31 fuses for interior and body electronics

## 2. Engine Bay Fuse Box (E-Box)

- **Location**: Right side of the engine bay, behind the strut tower
- **Access**: Release the clips on the E-box cover and lift off
- **Contents**: High-current fuses and relays for engine systems

## Additional Relay Locations

- **Behind glove box**: Additional relays mounted to the body
- **Trunk area**: Battery junction box for trunk-mounted battery fuse
- **Under dashboard (driver's side)**: ABS/DSC relay
