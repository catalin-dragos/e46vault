## Troubleshooting Tips

- Always replace fuses with the same amperage rating
- A repeatedly blown fuse indicates a short circuit — investigate the protected circuit
- Carry spare fuses in the vehicle (a fuse assortment is stored in the glove box fuse panel cover)
