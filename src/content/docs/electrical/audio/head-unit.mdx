The E46 was available with several head unit options depending on equipment level and model year.

## OEM Head Units

| Model | Type | Features |
|-------|------|----------|
| Business CD | Single-DIN | AM/FM, single CD |
| Business CD (facelift) | Single-DIN | Updated display |
| Professional Navigation | Double-DIN | Navigation, CD, wide screen |

## Aftermarket Replacement

The E46 uses a standard ISO/DIN radio cavity that accepts aftermarket head units with the appropriate adapter kit:
- **Wiring harness adapter**: Converts BMW-specific harness to ISO
- **Antenna adapter**: BMW uses a specific antenna connector
- **Steering wheel control adapter**: Maintains steering wheel button functionality

## Android Head Units

Several Android-based head units are available that fit the E46 dashboard perfectly, offering navigation, Bluetooth, Apple CarPlay, and Android Auto in an OEM-look form factor.
