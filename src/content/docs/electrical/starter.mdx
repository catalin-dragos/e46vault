The starter motor is mounted to the bell housing at the rear of the engine, engaging the flywheel ring gear to crank the engine.

## Specifications

- **Type**: Gear-reduction starter
- **Manufacturer**: Bosch
- **Location**: Between engine and transmission, accessible from below

## Failure Symptoms

- Clicking noise when turning key (solenoid engaging but motor not spinning)
- Grinding noise (worn Bendix gear)
- Slow cranking (worn motor or low battery)
- No response when turning key (check EWS, starter relay, and battery connections)

## Diagnosis Sequence

1. Check battery voltage (greater than 12.4V)
2. Check battery terminal connections (clean and tight)
3. Check starter relay (E-box)
4. Check EWS system (immobilizer must authorize start)
5. Test starter motor directly to rule out wiring

## Replacement Notes

- Access from underneath the vehicle
- Disconnect battery before working on starter
- Transmission may need to be supported slightly to gain access
