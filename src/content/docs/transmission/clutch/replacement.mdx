Clutch replacement on the E46 requires removing the transmission, exhaust, driveshaft, and heat shields.

## When to Replace

- Clutch slipping under load
- Clutch judder (vibration when engaging)
- Difficulty engaging gears
- High clutch engagement point
- Typically at 100,000-150,000 miles

## What to Replace

At minimum: clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing. Also recommended:
- Dual-mass flywheel (if over 80,000 miles)
- Pilot bearing
- Clutch slave cylinder
- Rear main seal (while accessible)
- Transmission mount
- Guibo and center support bearing

## Key Points

- Use a quality clutch kit (LuK or Sachs OEM)
- Align the clutch disc with an alignment tool
- Torque pressure plate bolts to 24 Nm in a star pattern
- Torque flywheel bolts to 105 Nm (new stretch bolts required)
