The clutch is hydraulically actuated using a master cylinder (at the pedal) and slave cylinder (at the transmission bell housing).

## Clutch Master Cylinder

- Located at the firewall, connected to the clutch pedal
- Shares brake fluid reservoir with the braking system
- Failure symptoms: soft or spongy clutch pedal

## Clutch Slave Cylinder
- Mounted on the transmission bell housing
- Replace preventively during clutch replacement

## Clutch Delay Valve (CDV)

The E46 has a clutch delay valve that slows clutch engagement.
- Many enthusiasts delete the CDV for more direct clutch feel
- CDV delete is a popular modification
