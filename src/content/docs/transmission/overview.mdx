The E46 330i and 330Ci were available with either a 5-speed manual (Getrag S6S-420G) or a 5-speed automatic (GM A5S 390R or ZF 5HP19) transmission. All RWD models use a front-engine, rear-drive layout with a two-piece driveshaft and rear-mounted differential.

## Transmission Options

| Transmission | Type | Gears | Models |
|-------------|------|-------|--------|
| Getrag S6S-420G | Manual | 5-speed | 330i, 330Ci |
| GM A5S 390R | Automatic | 5-speed | 330i, 330Ci (early) |
| ZF 5HP19 | Automatic | 5-speed | 330i, 330Ci (later) |

## Drivetrain Layout

- **Engine** → **Transmission** → **Driveshaft** (two-piece with center support bearing and guibo) → **Differential** → **Halfshafts** → **Rear wheels**

## Final Drive Ratios

| Configuration | Ratio |
|--------------|-------|
| Manual (330i/Ci) | 3.07:1 |
| Automatic (330i/Ci) | 3.46:1 |
| Manual (330xi) | 3.46:1 |
| Automatic (330xi) | 3.46:1 |
